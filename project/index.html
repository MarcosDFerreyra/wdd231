About the Band

Imagine Dragons is a globally acclaimed rock band formed in Las Vegas. Known for their powerful anthems, emotional lyrics, and dynamic performances, the band has become one of the most influential forces in modern rock. With chart-topping albums and unforgettable live shows, Imagine Dragons continues to inspire millions of fans around the world.
